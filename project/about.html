About ACAD Connect

From learning to earning — empowering Nigerian students to reach their goals.

Our Story

ACAD Connect was born out of a real need — Nigerian students with talent, ambition, and dreams were being held back by limited access to quality learning, mentorship, and job placement. We saw countless students completing degrees without the tools needed to thrive in the real world. So we decided to do something about it. Our journey began with a simple goal: to bridge the gap between education and opportunity. Today, ACAD Connect is more than just a platform — it’s a movement. We're empowering students with relevant skills, connecting them to real-world opportunities, and giving them the confidence to walk into interviews, freelance projects, or business pitches and own the room.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make high-quality, industry-relevant education accessible to every Nigerian student, no matter where they are. We want to equip you not just with knowledge, but with skills that pay — and help you build a future you're proud of.Whether you're trying to land your first job, build a portfolio, start freelancing, or just learn how to write a killer resume — ACAD Connect has your back.
